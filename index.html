About LTAutomation

LT Automation is a Puerto Rico-Based Company specialized in high technology system integration. Founded and incorporated in Puerto Rico in 2004 it has grown at a steady pace ever since. It serves the energy, water, refinery, and pharmaceutical markets in the Americas and the Caribbean by providing support and value-added solutions to operational data by maximizing the use of OSIsoft’s PI System®.
