About Us

LTAutomation is a Puerto Rico-Based Company specialized in high technology system integration. Founded and incorporated in Puerto Rico in 2004 it has grown at a steady pace ever since. It serves the energy, water, refinery, and pharmaceutical markets in the Americas and the Caribbean by providing support and value-added solutions to operational data by maximizing the use of OSIsoft’s PI System®. From sizing your PI System to installation, configuration, commissioning and making it work for your company by means of visualizations, reports and KPI’s, LTAutomation is your one-stop solution. Our end to end approach, product line, and expertise, will make your investment get the quickest ROI and help you accomplish your operational, maintenance and financial goals.

Our Team

LTAutomation’s management and engineering team have extensive hands-on experience in all aspects of project management, design, and implementation. Our areas of expertise include Process Automation, Control Systems, OSIsoft Installation, development and maintenance, Power Plant O&M, Water Utilities repair, and maintenance, among many others.
